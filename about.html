ABOUT- VIRGIN ASIA E-MARKETING PRIVATE LIMITED

Welcome to VirginAsia E-Marketing Private Limited, a dynamic and rapidly growing company established on November 7, 2023. Registered under the Corporate Identification Number (CIN) U41003KL2023PTC084426, we are a privately held, non-governmental company based in Ernakulam, Kerala. Our authorized share capital stands at ₹10,00,000, with a paid-up capital of ₹1,00,000.


At VirginAsia E-Marketing, we specialize in offering a wide range of industrial services to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our offerings include high-quality ship parts, construction equipment, and a variety of other industrial products and services. We pride ourselves on providing solutions that help businesses thrive across multiple sectors, particularly in construction and maritime industries.

Our company is spearheaded by two experienced directors: Retheesh Melepokattu Appukkuttan and Lakshmi Mangaladevi, whose leadership drives the company’s vision and commitment to excellence. We are registered with the Registrar of Companies (RoC-Ernakulam) and are actively involved in the construction of buildings and other related services.


Located at HD-08 55/1171/90A, Beyond, Kadavanthara, Ernakulam, Kerala, we are dedicated to delivering superior service, unmatched quality, and seamless solutions to our clients. VirginAsia E-Marketing continues to expand its portfolio of services, ensuring that we remain a trusted partner in the industries we serve.
